What is the significance of the bridge in a song?

The bridge is a section in a song that adds contrast and transitions between different parts of the song, providing a break and introducing new musical elements.

What is the purpose of a conductor in an orchestra?

The conductor leads and coordinates the orchestra, interpreting the music, guiding the tempo and dynamics, and ensuring the musicians play together as a unified ensemble.

What is the difference between a major and a minor key in music?

A major key sounds bright and happy, while a minor key sounds darker and sadder, creating different emotional qualities in the music.
